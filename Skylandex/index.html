⚡ About Skylandex

The Fusion of Two Worlds

Skylandex is a fan-made crossover wiki imagining a world where the elemental Skylanders of Skylands have been drawn into the realm of Pokémon battles. Each Skylander carries one of ten primal elements — Water, Fire, Earth, Life, Tech, Air, Magic, Undead, Light, and Dark — and these elements interact with each other just like Pokémon types.

Imagine Spyro's fire breath being doused by a Water-type counterattack, or Trigger Happy's golden Tech blasts short-circuiting in a rainstorm of Earth energy. Every battle in Skylandex is a strategic dance of elemental strengths and weaknesses, blending the adventurous spirit of Skylanders with the competitive depth of Pokémon.

This wiki is your Portal of Power into that world. Study the type chart, assemble your team of Skylanders, and prepare to face the Academy's Grand Elemental Tournament.

🔮 The Ten Elements

💧 Water

Masters of the seas and rivers. Fluid, adaptive, and powerful under pressure.

🔥 Fire

Born of volcanoes and passion. Their fury scorches through Tech and Undead alike.

🌍 Earth

Ancient and immovable. Earth Skylanders draw power from the world beneath their feet.

🌿 Life

Nature's champions. They heal allies and overwhelm Water and Earth.

⚙️ Tech

Gadgets and gizmos aplenty. Tech types dominate the skies and the seas.

🌀 Air

Swift and untouchable, except by Earth — which grounds even the mightiest gale.

✨ Magic

Raw arcane energy. Unpredictable and devastating against Undead and Dark.

💀 Undead

Risen from shadow. Undead Skylanders fear only Life — the force that denies them.

☀️ Light

Pure radiance incarnate. Light pierces through Dark and Magic's veil.

🌑 Dark

Shadows given form. Dark bends light and overwhelms Air, but fears Light itself.

📖 Lore

The Grand Tournament of Skylands

Long after Master Eon sealed the last Darkness rift, a strange phenomenon began rippling through the Portal network. Creatures from another dimension — small, impossibly varied, and crackling with elemental energy — began appearing across Skylands. The Skylanders called them "Eonmon", spirits of pure elemental will.

To restore order and understand this new world, Master Eon decreed the founding of the Skylandex Academy, where Skylanders could train alongside their Eonmon partners and compete in elemental tournaments. The rules? Simple. Each element has its strengths, its weaknesses, and its counters. Knowing them means the difference between victory and being sent back through the Portal.

The greatest Skylanders — those who can master all ten elements and read every matchup — are awarded the title of Grand Portal Master, the highest honor in both worlds.
